## 连分数展开

求 $\sqrt{2}$ 的值。

∵ $$\because \sqrt{2}=1+x$$

∴ $$\therefore x=\sqrt{2}-1$$

$$\because (\sqrt{2}-1)\times(\sqrt{2}+1)=1$$

$$\therefore \sqrt{2}-1=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}+1}=\frac{1}{2 + \sqrt{2}-1}=\frac{1}{2 + \frac{1}{2 + \sqrt{2}-1}}$$

$$\therefore \sqrt{2}=1+x=1+\frac{1}{2 + \frac{1}{2 + \sqrt{2}-1}} = 1.5$$
